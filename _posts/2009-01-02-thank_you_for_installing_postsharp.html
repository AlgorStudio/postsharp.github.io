Thank you for installing PostSharp!

We are pleased to welcome you in the postsharp.org community! Please take a minute to check support options, register to newsletters or RSS, and learn how to contribute to the project.

  • Subscribe to the PostSharp Official Blog for notifications of new releases, events and usefull information. 
  • Check out our support options. We provide both community support and professional support. Don't stay with unanswered questions!
  • Spread the word! Blog about PostSharp, present it to your user group: We have presentation material available for you...  or invite us to do so in your user group.
  • Submit your contribution to the community.
